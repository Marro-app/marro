# Marro Ambassador Guide

Thanks for being one of the first people spreading the word about Marro. Here's everything you need to know.

## What Marro is, today

Marro turns your financial aid letter into a monthly plan you can actually stick to — Total aid in, a clear budget out, so you know what you can spend and what's left as Planned surplus if you stay on budget. Loan tracking is on the way, but it's not live yet. Marro is completely free for med students right now.

## How to invite people

Open **Invite Friends** in the app to get started:

- Tap **Generate code** to create an invite code. You have **15 invites** to give out.
- The easiest way to share one: tap the email icon next to a code and send it straight to your friend. Marro emails them a link that automatically fills in the invite code for them — no typing, no copy-pasting required on their end.
- You can also just copy the code and send it yourself (text, DM, whatever) if that's easier — your friend can enter it by hand instead.
- Each code works for one person, one time. Once it's used, it's used.
- You can revoke a code you haven't used yet if you change your mind about who to send it to.

## Where to send feedback

Anything you notice — bugs, confusing screens, feature ideas, anything — send it to **hello@joinmarro.com**. We read everything.

## What we're asking of you

About 15 minutes a week, that's it. When you get a chance, let us know:

- What confused the people you invited when they first used Marro.
- What questions they asked about their aid letters or their budget.
- Whether anyone came back and opened the app a second time on their own (this tells us a lot).

That's it — just keep an eye out and pass along what you notice. And to be clear: we never sell your personal info, or your friends'. Thanks for helping us build this right.
